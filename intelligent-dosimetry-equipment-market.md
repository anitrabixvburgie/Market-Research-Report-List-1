<p><p>The Intelligent Dosimetry Equipment Market includes advanced technologies like Thermoluminescent Dosimetry (TLD) and Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL). TLD uses heat to release stored energy from radiation exposure, providing accurate dose measurements. In contrast, OSL employs light to stimulate trapped electrons in specific materials, offering high sensitivity and reusability for dose assessment. Both methods are essential in radiation monitoring for healthcare, nuclear, and industrial applications, ensuring safety and compliance with exposure regulations while enhancing precision in dose measurement.</p></p>
