<p><p>Battery grade manganese sulfate is produced through various processes, predominantly defined by their source materials. The "Process Route: from Electrolytic Manganese" focuses on deriving manganese sulfate from electrolytic manganese metal, providing high purity levels. Additionally, "Resources Recovery" involves recycling manganese from industrial waste or spent batteries, promoting sustainability and resource efficiency in the manganese supply chain.</p></p>
